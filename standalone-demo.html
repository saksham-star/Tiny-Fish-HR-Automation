About TinyFish HR Automation

The Problem

Recruitment teams spend 25-30 hours per week on manual work that could be automated:

  • 📋 Job posting aggregation (8 hrs/week)
  • 📄 Resume screening (12 hrs/week)
  • 📅 Interview scheduling (6 hrs/week)

Our Solution

Multi-step web automation that handles real complexity:

  • 🔗 Navigate real websites (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor)
  • 🔐 Handle authentication (OAuth, SSO, form login)
  • 📊 Extract structured data (jobs, resumes, candidate info)
  • ⚙️ Manage state across 50+ steps
  • 📧 Automate calendar & email workflows

Business Value

  • 💰 $40,560 saved per customer per year
  • 📉 30-40% reduction in hiring cycle time
  • ✅ Better candidate quality through consistent screening
  • 🎯 $3M+ ARR potential at scale
